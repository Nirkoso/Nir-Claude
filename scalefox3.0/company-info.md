## Company Overview

**Name:** ScaleFox.ai
**Industry:** AI-powered Marketing Automation & Competitive Intelligence
**Primary Focus:** LinkedIn Advertising for B2B Companies
**Tagline:** "Turn your competitor's success into your next ad campaign"

ScaleFox.ai is an AI-powered marketing automation platform that specializes in competitive intelligence-driven LinkedIn advertising. The platform helps busy founders and marketers create winning ad campaigns by analyzing competitor ads and automatically generating optimized campaigns in under 5 minutes.

---

## What ScaleFox Does

### Core Product: AI-Powered LinkedIn Ad Campaign Generator

ScaleFox automates the entire campaign creation workflow through four steps:

1. **Research** - Analyzes competitor ads, landing pages, and social presence
2. **Analyze** - Extracts winning patterns and best practices from competitor campaigns
3. **Strategize** - Creates comprehensive GTM (Go-To-Market) playbooks based on insights
4. **Create** - Generates ready-to-launch LinkedIn ad campaigns

### Key Features

- **ICP Persona Research** - Automatically identifies and researches Ideal Customer Profile personas, including goals, pain points, messaging preferences, and social habits
- **Competitive Intelligence Engine** - Scrapes and analyzes competitor advertising across platforms to identify successful patterns
- **GTM Playbook Creation** - Synthesizes competitor insights into strategic frameworks and campaign approaches
- **AI Ad Generation** - Creates customizable LinkedIn ads optimized based on proven competitor strategies
- **Speed to Market** - Reduces campaign creation from days/hours to under 5 minutes

## Product Workflow

### User Journey (5 Minutes Total)

**Step 1: Company Input** (30 seconds)
- User enters their company URL
- ScaleFox analyzes company info, industry, value proposition

**Step 2: Competitor Analysis** (1 minute)
- User provides competitor names or URLs
- AI scrapes and analyzes competitor ads, landing pages, social presence

**Step 3: Persona Selection** (1 minute)
- ScaleFox generates ICP personas based on company + competitor data
- User selects or customizes target personas

**Step 4: GTM Playbook Generation** (1 minute)
- AI synthesizes insights into strategic framework
- Creates messaging themes, positioning angles, creative approaches

**Step 5: Ad Creation** (2 minutes)
- AI generates multiple ad variations based on GTM playbook
- User customizes copy, images, CTAs
- Exports to LinkedIn Campaign Manager
