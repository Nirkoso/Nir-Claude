# LinkedIn Campaign Structure Framework

## Standard Campaign Phases

### 2-Week Sprint Structure
Use for budgets < $2,000

**Phase 1: Test (Days 1-7)**
- Purpose: Validate messaging and audience
- Budget: 40% of total
- Approach: Equal split across test variants
- Success: Identify 1-2 winning combinations

**Phase 2: Optimize (Days 8-14)**
- Purpose: Scale winners, improve efficiency
- Budget: 60% of total
- Approach: 70% to winners, 30% to optimization
- Success: Hit target CPL

---

### Monthly Campaign Structure
Use for budgets ≥ $2,000

**Phase 1: Test (Days 1-7)**
- Purpose: Validate messaging and audience
- Budget: 30% of total
- Approach: Test 3-6 concepts across personas
- Success: Find winning formula

**Phase 2: Optimize (Days 8-21)**
- Purpose: Refine targeting and creative
- Budget: 35% of total
- Approach: A/B test optimizations
- Success: Improve efficiency by 20%+

**Phase 3: Scale (Days 22-30)**
- Purpose: Maximize conversions
- Budget: 35% of total
- Approach: Full budget to validated approach
- Success: Achieve volume goals

---

## Phase Transition Gates

### Exit Test Phase
✅ At least 1 concept with CPL < target
✅ Minimum 10 conversions total
✅ Statistical significance (7+ days data)

❌ If not met: Extend test phase +3 days OR adjust targets

### Exit Optimize Phase
✅ Consistent CPL within 10% of target
✅ Stable daily conversion volume
✅ Creative fatigue not detected

❌ If not met: Refresh creative before scaling

---

## Budget Reserve

Always hold 5-10% in reserve for:
- Unexpected opportunities (viral content)
- Emergency optimizations
- Phase extensions
- Competitive responses

**Formula:** Reserve = Total Budget × 0.075 (7.5%)

---

## Campaign Naming Convention

`[Objective]-[Persona]-[Month][Year]`

Examples:
- `LeadGen-VP_Marketing-Jan25`
- `Awareness-Growth_Marketers-Q1_25`

---

## Daily Monitoring Schedule

**Days 1-3:** Check 2x daily (morning/evening)
- Watch for delivery issues
- Verify tracking works

**Days 4-7:** Check daily
- Track early performance trends
- Flag any anomalies

**Days 8+:** Check every other day
- Review phase performance
- Make optimization decisions
