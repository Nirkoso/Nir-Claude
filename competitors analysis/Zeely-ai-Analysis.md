## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Zeely.ai is a Ukrainian-founded, Delaware-based AI-powered advertising platform that has rapidly scaled to 650,000+ active users since launching in August 2022. The company positions itself as a mobile-first, all-in-one advertising solution that enables small businesses and e-commerce brands to create high-converting ad campaigns in under 30 minutes.

While Zeely shares some surface-level similarities with ScaleFox.ai in terms of AI-powered ad creation and speed-to-market, the companies serve fundamentally different market segments with distinct value propositions. Zeely targets e-commerce merchants and SMBs seeking visual creative generation across multiple platforms (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Google, LinkedIn), with heavy emphasis on UGC-style video content featuring AI avatars. In contrast, ScaleFox specializes in B2B marketers requiring competitive intelligence-driven LinkedIn campaigns with GTM playbook approaches.

Critical vulnerabilities identified in Zeely's business include polarized customer sentiment (42% one-star reviews vs. 40% five-star reviews on Trustpilot), significant billing and subscription issues, AI accuracy problems, and limited customization capabilities. Their pricing model ($29.95-$79.95/month) positions them as a budget-friendly option but creates sustainability questions given the capital-intensive nature of AI development. ScaleFox maintains clear competitive advantages in B2B positioning, competitive intelligence integration, and strategic GTM frameworks that Zeely does not address.

20. **Don't Ignore Zeely's Weaknesses Publicly**
    - Tempting to attack competitor's 42% one-star reviews
    - Better: Emphasize ScaleFox's strengths (reliability, accuracy, trust) without naming Zeely
    - Let prospects discover Zeely's issues through their own research
