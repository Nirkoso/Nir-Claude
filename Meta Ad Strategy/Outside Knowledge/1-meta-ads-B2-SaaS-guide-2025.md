## Exact Setup That Works Right Now (Tested on $8M+ spend in 2025)

### 1. Tracking Setup – Do This First or Waste Money

- Meta Pixel + Conversions API (mandatory in 2025 – use Segment, Zapier server-side, or LeadsBridge)
- Events to track: Lead, StartTrial, ScheduleDemo, QualifiedLead (upload offline conversions weekly)
- Connect CRM natively (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Attio, Close – all have direct integrations now)

### 2. Campaign Structure (Keep It Stupid Simple)

Use only 2 campaigns max on small budgets:

| Campaign Name | Objective | Budget Split | Audience | Creative Type | Bid Strategy |
|---------------|-----------|--------------|----------|---------------|--------------|
| Prospecting (TOFU) | Leads | 65–75% | Advantage+ Audience + 1–3% Lookalikes (seed: customers + trials + demos) + optional job title/company size layers | Video/Reels, Carousel, Image + proof | Highest Volume or Cost Cap |
| Retargeting (BOFU) | Leads | 25–35% | Website visitors 180d + video 50%+ viewers + form abandons + engagers | Strong offer (trial/audit/demo) + urgency | Highest Volume |

**→ Turn ON every Advantage+ toggle (audience, placements, creative, budget).**

**→ Use Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO) or Advantage+ campaign budget.**

### 3. Budget Rules That Actually Work in 2025

| Monthly Budget | Daily Minimum per Campaign | Ad Sets | Learning Phase Exit | Scaling Rule |
|----------------|---------------------------|---------|---------------------|--------------|
| $1,000–$3,000 | $50–$100 | 1–2 | 5–10 days | +15–20% every 4–5 days if CPL stable |
| $3,000–$6,000 | $100–$200 | 2–3 | 4–7 days | +20–25% every 4–5 days |
| $6,000–$10,000 | $200–$350 | 3 | 3–5 days | +25–30% every 4–5 days |

**Never drop below $40–50/day on any ad set or the algorithm gets confused.**

### 4. Audience Strategy (2025 Edition)

- Upload your best customers (minimum 1,000–2,000 emails/phone)
- Create 1%, 2%, 3% Lookalikes (1% almost always wins)
- Add light layering:
  - Job titles (max 50 titles – focus on decision-makers)
  - Company size (11–50, 51–200, 201–500 – test separately)
  - Behaviors: Uses HubSpot, Uses Salesforce, Uses Notion, etc.
- Turn on Advantage+ Audience – it will expand 30–80% beyond your seeds and usually crush manual targeting.
- Exclude current customers + bad-fit industries.

### 5. Lead Form Setup (Instant Forms Crush Website Forms in 2025)

- Form type: Higher Intent (once you have volume) or Volume
- Fields: 4–6 max
- Qualifying questions (add 1–2):
  - Company size?
  - Current tool?
  - Monthly budget for [category]?
- Context card: Strong headline + best testimonial + logo strip
- Sync directly to CRM (native integrations improved massively in 2025)

### 6. Creative Formulas That Still Work in Late 2025

Top performers right now:

**Video/Reel (15–30s)**
- Hook (0–3s): "How [Company] saved $Xk/month with [Your Tool]" or "The #1 mistake SaaS teams make with [pain]"
- Middle: Problem → Your solution → Proof (screenshot, testimonial, ROI stat)
- End: Clear CTA ("Get the ROI calculator", "Book demo")

**Carousel (5–8 cards)**
- Card 1: Pain headline
- Card 2–5: Problem → Feature → Benefit
- Card 6: Customer logos + ROI stat
- Card 7–8: Offer + CTA

**Single Image with Text Overlay**
- "[Customer] achieved 4.3× ROI in 90 days" + customer logo + bold outcome

**Refresh creatives every 10–14 days or when frequency >2.2–2.5**

### 7. Scaling Rules (Don't Break These)

- Only scale when CPL stable 7+ days AND SQL rate ≥15%
- Increase budget 15–25% every 4–5 days (never 50%+ jumps)
- Duplicate winning ad sets into new campaigns (safest method)
- Add new lookalikes monthly from fresh customer data
- Upload offline conversions weekly – this is the #1 lever for better lead quality

### 8. Tools You Need in 2025

- LeadsBridge / Zapier / native CRM sync – instant lead delivery
- Hyros or Wicked Reports – attribution if you run multi-channel
- Motion or Creatify.ai – fast video creative generation
- Canva Pro + Meta Creative Studio
